Explored bike-sharing demand patterns in London using **Python** and **Tableau**. Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis on **17K+ records**, analyzed weather impact, and built interactive dashboards.

Identified peak demand at **7 AM and 5 PM** with a strong dependency on weather conditions, providing actionable insights for resource allocation and operational planning.
